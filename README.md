Navbar (Top Bar)
Fixed at the top — always visible as you scroll. Shows "Hotel Sagar" logo with "Dombivli East, Mumbai" underneath. Links to Rooms, Restaurant, Gallery, Reviews, and a highlighted "Contact Us" button in clay red.

Hero Section (First thing you see)
Dark rich brown background with a subtle dot pattern overlay — gives a warm, premium desi feel. Left side has the headline "Where Every Stay Feels Like Home" with "Home" in italic clay orange. Below that a short description and two buttons — "View Rooms" and "See Our Menu." Right side shows a card with real stats: 4.5★ rating, 639+ happy guests, 24/7 service — all pulled from actual Google Maps data.

Rooms Section
Three room cards on a cream background — Deluxe (₹1,200/night), Premium (₹1,800/night), and Executive Suite (₹2,800/night). Each card has a colored gradient image placeholder, room type label, description, amenity tags like AC / WiFi / Smart TV, and the price. Cards lift up slightly when you hover.
Restaurant / Menu Section
Dark bark-brown background. 4 clickable tabs — Breakfast, Main Course, Snacks, Drinks. Click a tab and the menu items switch instantly. Items show a green dot for veg and red dot for non-veg, dish name, description, and price. Full desi menu — poha, thali, mutton curry, vada pav, masala chai, lassi, everything.
Gallery Section

A 3-column photo grid with colored placeholders showing emoji icons for rooms, food, and ambience. Once the owner gives you real photos, you just swap them in.
Reviews Section
Three real guest reviews taken directly from Hotel Sagar's Google listing — displayed as cards with quote marks, star ratings, and reviewer names.
Contact Section

Dark background. Left side has the real address, real phone number (clickable to call), and opening hours. Plus a green WhatsApp button that opens a direct chat with a pre-typed message. Right side has a map placeholder with a "Open in Google Maps" link pointing to the actual hotel location.

Footer
Simple dark footer with the hotel name, location, and a small credit line.
